  • How do I book a gas cylinder?

    You can book a gas cylinder by visiting our website and following the steps to register and login. Once you are logged in, you can select the date and time you want to pick up the cylinder and pay for it.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and Paytm QR codes.

  • What if I need to cancel my booking?

    You can cancel your booking by contacting our customer service team at least 24 hours in advance. If you cancel within 24 hours of the pickup time, you will be charged a cancellation fee is Rs 50.

  • What if I am not available to pick up the cylinder at the scheduled time?

    If you are not available to pick up the cylinder at the scheduled time, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. We will try to reschedule the pickup or arrange for delivery.

Delivery

  • How much does delivery cost?

    Delivery costs depend on the location and the size of the cylinder. You can find the delivery charges on our website before you place your order.

  • How long will it take for my delivery to arrive?

    Delivery times vary depending on the location. In general, deliveries should take between 1 and 3 hours, but this can vary depending on traffic and weather conditions.

  • What if I am not home when the delivery arrives?

    If you are not home when the delivery arrives, our delivery team will leave the cylinder at your doorstep and leave a note with instructions on how to retrieve it. You can also contact our customer service team to reschedule the delivery.

Safety

  • Are your cylinders safe to use?

    Yes, our cylinders are safe to use and meet all industry standards. They are regularly inspected and tested to ensure they are in good condition.

  • What safety precautions should I take when using a gas cylinder?

    When using a gas cylinder, you should always follow the safety guidelines provided by the manufacturer. These include storing the cylinder properly, handling it with care, and using it in a well-ventilated area. You should also make sure you have the necessary training and experience to use a gas cylinder before doing so.

  • What should I do if I suspect a cylinder is leaking or malfunctioning?

    If you suspect a cylinder is leaking or malfunctioning, you should immediately turn off the gas and exit the area. Do not attempt to repair or modify the cylinder in any way. Contact our customer service team for assistance.
